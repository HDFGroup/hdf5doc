The historical files in this Supplements/ directory were added to the HDF5 
documentation repository in January 2012.  Most are design documents or
drafts of user documentation from the Release 1.8.0 development cycle.  
These files will be moved or removed once the content is confirmed to have 
been incorporated into the formal HDF5 documentation.
