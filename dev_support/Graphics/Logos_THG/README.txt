
This portion of the repository contains The HDF Group's logos.

Logos are available in the following color schemes:
    Blue and green -- normally used in a color-capable environment
    Black          -- normally used in a black-and-white envrionment
    Rainbow        -- not used (and do not use)
    White          -- EPS only; not used to date

Logos are available in two sizes and in the following formats:
    EPS
    JPEG
    TIFF (for PC or Mac)

Logos are available with and without the text 'The HDF Group' 
immediately below the 'HDF' image.  This is indicated by the 'text' 
and 'notext' strings in the filenames.
